On Balance Volume (OBV)

MarketTechnicals.obv — Function.
obv(ohlcv; price="Close", v="Volume")

On Balance Volume

\[ OBV_t = OBV_{t - 1} + \begin{cases} volume & \text{if} \ close_t > close_{t-1} \\ 0 & \text{if} \ close_t = close_{t-1} \\ -volume & \text{if} \ close_t < close_{t-1} \end{cases}\]

MarketTechnicals.vwap — Function.
vwap(ohlcv, n; price="Close", v="Volume")

Volume Weight-Adjusted Price

\[ P = \frac{\sum_j P_j Q_j}{\sum_j Q_j} \ ,\text{where Q is the volume}\]
