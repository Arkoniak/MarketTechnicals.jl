Accumulation/Distribution Line (ADL)

MarketTechnicals.adl — Function
adl(ohlcv; h="High", l="Low", c="Close", v="Volume")

Accumulation/Distribution Line

Developed by Marc Chaikin.

Formula

\[ ADL_t = ADL_{t-1} + 'frac{(Close_t - Low_t) - (High_t - Close_t)}{High_t - Low_t}' 'times Volume_t'\]

On Balance Volume (OBV)

MarketTechnicals.obv — Function
obv(ohlcv; price="Close", v="Volume")

On Balance Volume

\[ OBV_t = OBV_{t - 1} + 'begin{cases} volume & text{if} close_t > close_{t-1} \ 0 & text{if} close_t = close_{t-1} \ -volume & text{if} close_t < close_{t-1} end{cases}'\]
